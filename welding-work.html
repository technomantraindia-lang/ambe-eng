Service Overview

Certified Welding, Zero Joint Flaws

At Ambe Engineering Co., we specialize in premium welding services for custom sheet metal fabrications and heavy structural framing. Our team of experienced, code-qualified welders utilizes advanced inverter-based MIG (Metal Inert Gas), high-frequency TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas), and spot welding machines to meet high-strength requirements.

By matching specific gas mixtures, filler wires, and heat profiles to each metal alloy, we ensure high penetration, zero slag inclusions, and minimal distortion on thin-gauge enclosures as well as heavy machinery columns.
