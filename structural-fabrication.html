Service Overview

Heavy Structural Steel, Uncompromising Strength

At Ambe Engineering Co., we specialize in processing and assembling industrial-grade structural systems. Our heavy fabrication bays handle standard steel sections including I-beams, channels, angles, hollow pipes, and thick plates, converting raw structures into robust factory plant framing, loading platforms, pipelines, and equipment supports.

Every structural weld is executed by certified welders in compliance with AWS/ASME guidelines, ensuring maximum fatigue resistance and load-carrying safety under dynamic factory conditions.

All welds undergo visual check guidelines. Critical structural joints undergo dye-penetrant (DP) tests to check for surface flaws, and third-party Ultrasonic Testing (UT) can be arranged.
