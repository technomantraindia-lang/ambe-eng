Service Overview

Plant Equipment, Precision Engineered Assemblies

At Ambe Engineering Co., we specialize in fabricating custom assemblies for plant utilities and machinery setups. Our engineering desk coordinates with your utility drawings to design and manufacture heavy machinery bases, conveyor frames, industrial guards, storage tanks, ventilation ductings, and custom mounting panels.

By using a combination of thick plate profiling, CNC press brake forming, and high-strength MIG/TIG welding, we produce components capable of continuous heavy-duty cycles with anti-corrosive industrial surface treatments.
