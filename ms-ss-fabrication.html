Service Overview

Premium Metal Selection, Flawless Finishing

At Ambe Engineering Co., we deliver high-quality fabrication services across mild steel and stainless steel alloys. Mild steel is processed primarily for robust structures, frames, brackets, and fixtures, finished with protective powder coatings or galvanizing. Stainless steel is profiled for pharmaceutical, food, dairy, and architectural components that demand high corrosion resistance, aesthetic satin/mirror finishes, and hygienic clean welds.

Our workshop maintains separated fabrication sections for carbon steel and stainless steel to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that SS parts remain fully corrosion-free after pickling and passivating.

Why Choose Us

Advantages of MS & SS Fabrication

Applying dedicated workspace parameters to preserve material purity and finish quality.

Anti-Contamination Work

SS fabrication is handled in a separate bay with SS-only tooling, preventing carbon iron contamination and rust spots.

Hygienic TIG Welds

Argon gas purging during TIG welding ensures full penetration and smooth crevice-free joints, essential for food/pharma sectors.

Aesthetic Polishing

We polish stainless steel structures to mirror or uniform satin hairline textures, ideal for architectural fittings.

Optimal Tonnage

Heavy MS frames are built using heavy-tonnage press bending and structural beams, offering robust long-term load capacity.
