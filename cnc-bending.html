Service Overview

Intricate Bending, Perfect Repeatability

At Ambe Engineering Co., we utilize advanced multi-axis CNC hydraulic bending machines to process sheet metal components with strict dimensional controls. Our automated press brakes ensure that every bend angle, flange depth, and radius matches design blueprints exactly, whether forming simple channels or complex multi-bend electronic enclosures.

By using precision-ground punch and die tooling configurations, we prevent surface cracking or excessive thinning of thin-gauge sheets and heavy structural plates alike. Our bending center caters to prototyping, custom batches, and high-volume mass production runs.
