Service Overview

Micron-Level Tolerance, High-Volume Consistency

At Ambe Engineering Co., we specialize in fabricating custom engineering components for automotive, industrial machinery, construction, and power sectors. Utilizing high-power CNC laser cutting and calibrated multi-angle press bending, we profile metal brackets, engine mounts, heavy washers, hinge plates, and custom flanges from diverse sheet gauges.

Our in-house CAM software nested layouts optimize plate yield, reducing scrap rate and lowering per-part manufacturing costs while delivering uniform edge finishes ready for line assembly.
