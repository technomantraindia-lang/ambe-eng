Service Overview

Maximum Precision, Minimum Waste

At Ambe Engineering Co., we use advanced fiber optic laser technology to deliver ultra-precise cutting solutions across a wide range of materials. Our computer-controlled laser heads focus high-density thermal energy onto sheet metals, instantly vaporizing the path to produce micro-tolerance profiles without structural stress or mechanical distortion.

Whether you require prototype development, low-volume custom parts, or high-volume sheet metal blanks, our fabrication center guarantees uniform edge quality and minimal scrap rate through optimized nesting layouts.

Why Choose Us

Advantages of Fiber Laser

Combining modern thermal profiling with our strict ISO 9001:2015 certified quality inspection standards.

Micro-Tolerances

Achieve intricate cuts and sharp corner geometries down to 0.1mm dimensional deviation, eliminating manual filing or secondary milling.

No Thermal Distortion

Fiber optic delivery directs heat purely at the focal point, minimizing the heat-affected zone (HAZ) and preventing thin-gauge sheet metal warping.

Flawless Edge Quality

High-pressure auxiliary gas (Nitrogen/Oxygen) blows away slag during penetration, leaving a clean, dross-free edge ready for immediate welding.

Nesting Efficiency

Computer-optimized nesting layout matches contours together closely, reducing waste scrap material and lowering overall unit cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about sheet specifications, order procedures, or manufacturing times?

What materials can you cut?

We routinely laser cut Mild Steel (MS), Stainless Steel (SS), and Aluminum. Our machines can also handle brass and copper sheets up to 6mm thick.

What is your turnaround time?

Production turnaround typically ranges from 5 to 10 business days depending on volume and nesting design complexity. Rush orders can be requested.

Do you provide design/CAD services?

Yes. If you do not have CAD drawings (.dxf, .dwg), our drafting team can convert physical sketches, hand-drawn diagrams, or PDF concepts into files.

What is the minimum order quantity?

We cater to both single prototype fabrication trials and high-volume mass production runs. There is no strict minimum order limit.

Are your processes certified?

Yes, we are an ISO 9001:2015 certified metal engineering company, meaning all processing, dimensional checking, and traceability meet international QA standards.
