Service Overview

End-to-End Processing, Precision Quality

At Ambe Engineering Co., we deliver complete sheet metal fabrication solutions, taking your project from concept drawings to fully-finished metal assemblies. Our workspace features integrated processes including high-power CNC laser cutting, press brake bending, precision welding (MIG/TIG), hardware insertion, and surface treatment.

Whether you require standard mild steel brackets, corrosion-resistant stainless steel housings, or light-weight aluminum guards, our engineering team ensures optimized layouts, high structural integrity, and perfect fitment during assembly trials.
